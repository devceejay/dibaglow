Pre-Treatment & Aftercare Tips

Your DibaGlow experience doesn't start or end at the spa, it's a holistic journey. These simple tips will help you get the most out of your visit and maintain your radiant results long after you leave.

Before Your Treatment

  • Hydrate Well: Drink plenty of water before your appointment to help your skin absorb the full benefits of your treatment.
  • Arrive with a Clean Face: If possible, come without makeup or heavy skincare products. This allows us to begin your glow ritual immediately and tailor products to your skin's natural state.
  • Avoid Harsh Treatments: Hold off on retinol, exfoliants, or chemical peels at least 72 hours before your session to avoid irritation.
  • Share Your Skin Story: We love details. Let us know about allergies, sensitivities, medications, or recent treatments so we can customize your experience.

After Your Treatment

  • Skip the Makeup (for a bit): Let your skin breathe and absorb the benefits. Wait at least 24 hours before applying makeup.
  • Stay Hydrated: Continue drinking water to flush toxins and enhance your glow from within.
  • Protect Your Skin: Use a gentle SPF daily, especially after facials or exfoliating treatments. Your skin will be more sensitive to sunlight.
  • Go Easy on Exfoliation: Give your skin 3-5 days to rest before using any scrubs, acids, or active treatments again.
  • Embrace the Ritual: Skincare is a journey. For long-lasting results, consistency is key, and we're here to guide you every step of the way.
